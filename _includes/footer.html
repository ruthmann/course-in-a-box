What is the NYU MusEDLab?

The NYU Music Experience Design Lab (MusEDLab) researches and designs technologies and experiences for music making, learning and engagement. We co-design together with community, industry, non-profit, educator and youth partners, with a focus on promoting creative making and learning, and lowering barriers to creative expression. Follow our work online at http://musedlab.org.
